Let's Play

Instructions:-

  1. Click on the "Start" button to play a game.
  2. Once you start a game you would have 30 seconds to play.Once time is out,you will be shown your final score as well.click on the "Start" button to restart the game.
  3. You are supposed to jump when circle object comes towards rectangle.
    To avoid the collision, click on the rectangle to get it jumped.
  4. Once the objects collide,your score and time are reset to it's initial stage. click on the "Start" button to restart the game.
  5. During play time,if you want to pause the game then click on the "Stop" button.
  6. After stopping ,if you start a game by clicking on Start button, be mindful that circle will start running from the last place before you pressed "Stop" button.
  7. Refresh the page to restart the entire game.
  8. You can change the color of the running object by inputting the color in the input field
